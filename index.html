About the Center

The National Center for Climate Change supports Uzbekistan’s transition to a resilient, low-emission development pathway.

We coordinate cross-sector efforts, develop evidence and analytics, and work with national and international partners to deliver measurable outcomes.

NDC implementation MRV / BTR Early warning Climate finance Air • water • land
Mission
Evidence-based climate policy support and delivery of national priorities through data, coordination, and implementation capacity.
Focus
Mitigation, adaptation, loss & damage, and mainstreaming climate across sectors and investments.
Approach
Practical toolkits, dashboards, project pipelines, and institutional strengthening with partners.
Results
Transparent metrics, reproducible methods, and quality assurance to track progress over time.
